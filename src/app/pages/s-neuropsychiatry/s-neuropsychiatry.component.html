Neuropsychiatry Department
Holistic Mental Health & Neurological Care

At Lamba Multi-Speciality Hospital, our Neuropsychiatry Department is committed to understanding and treating mental health disorders and neurological conditions through a comprehensive, evidence-based approach. We blend traditional counseling with advanced neurotherapies to offer holistic healing solutions.

Our Sub-Divisions & Services
Mental Health Counseling and Therapy

Our experts provide one-on-one and group therapy sessions to help patients manage stress, trauma, anxiety, and relationship challenges. Using techniques such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), we help individuals regain mental well-being and emotional stability.

Suicide Prevention and Crisis Intervention

We provide immediate support and long-term counseling for individuals struggling with suicidal thoughts, depression, or severe emotional distress. Our emergency response team ensures timely intervention and a supportive recovery process.

Alcohol and Drug De-Addiction Programs

Addiction can impact every aspect of life. Our personalized de-addiction programs focus on:

  • Medical detoxification to manage withdrawal symptoms.
  • Behavioral therapy to address psychological dependency.
  • Relapse prevention programs for sustained recovery.
Treatment for Depression, Anxiety, and Bipolar Disorder

Our specialists use a combination of medication, psychotherapy, and lifestyle modifications to help patients manage:

  • Chronic depression and mood disorders
  • Generalized anxiety disorder (GAD)
  • Bipolar disorder and personality disorders
Advanced Technology & Equipment
EEG (Electroencephalogram)

Monitors brain activity to diagnose conditions like epilepsy, seizures, and neuropsychiatric disorders.

Biofeedback Machines

Helps patients control physiological responses such as stress and anxiety using real-time feedback techniques.

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)

A non-invasive therapy for treating depression and neurological disorders.

Digital Detox Programs

Designed to help individuals struggling with excessive screen time, gaming, or social media addiction.
