Orthopedics Department
Restoring Mobility, Enhancing Lives

At Lamba Multi-Speciality Hospital, our Orthopedics Department is dedicated to diagnosing, treating, and managing a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions affecting the bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. We offer comprehensive care through both surgical and non-surgical approaches, ensuring that each patient receives a personalized treatment plan tailored to their needs.

Comprehensive Orthopedic Services
Joint Replacement Surgery

We specialize in total and partial knee, hip, and shoulder replacements, using advanced implant technology and minimally invasive techniques for quicker recovery and lasting results.

Arthroscopy & Sports Medicine

Our minimally invasive keyhole surgeries help diagnose and treat sports injuries, ligament tears (ACL, MCL), meniscus injuries, and joint disorders with minimal downtime.

Spine Care & Surgery

Our spine specialists offer advanced treatment for herniated discs, spinal deformities, sciatica, and degenerative spine conditions, using microscopic and minimally invasive techniques for better spinal health.

Fracture & Trauma Management

We provide emergency care for fractures, dislocations, and orthopedic trauma with state-of-the-art fracture fixation, bone grafting, and rehabilitation services.
