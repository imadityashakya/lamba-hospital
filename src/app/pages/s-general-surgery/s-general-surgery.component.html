General Surgery Department
Excellence in Surgical Care

At Lamba Multi-Speciality Hospital, our General Surgery Department offers expert surgical care, from routine procedures to complex surgeries, using the latest in minimally invasive techniques. Our focus is on precision, safety, and faster recovery to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

Our Sub-Divisions & Services
Hernia Repairs

Our skilled surgeons perform open and laparoscopic hernia repairs, ensuring long-term relief and reduced risk of recurrence.

Appendectomies

Emergency appendicitis surgery is performed with laparoscopic precision, reducing complications and promoting a speedy recovery.

Laparoscopic Surgeries

We specialize in minimally invasive laparoscopic procedures, which involve small incisions, quicker recovery, and minimal post-operative pain. Common laparoscopic surgeries include

  • Gallbladder removal (Cholecystectomy)
  • Appendectomy (Appendix removal)
  • Hernia repairs
Treatment of Gastrointestinal Disorders

Our department handles complex GI surgeries, including:

  • Bowel obstruction surgery
  • Ulcer treatment and gastrectomies
  • Hemorrhoidectomy for piles and anorectal disorders
Advanced Technology & Equipment
Laparoscopic Towers

Endoscopy & Colonoscopy Units

Modular Operation Theaters

Equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure safety and efficiency in every surgery.
