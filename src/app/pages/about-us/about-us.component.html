Our Legacy
Meet our founder

Founded in 1986 by the visionary Dr. R.S. Lamba, Lamba Multispeciality Hospital holds the distinction of being North India's first private hospital dedicated to psychiatric care. Over the years, we have continuously evolved, expanding our services and upgrading our infrastructure to meet the growing needs of our community. In 2021, we undertook a transformative renovation, emerging as a state-of-the-art multispeciality hospital.

Our Mission

At Lamba Multi-Speciality Hospital, our mission is to provide exceptional, patient-centered healthcare by integrating state-of-the-art medical technology with compassionate care. We are dedicated to improving lives by offering comprehensive, affordable, and high-quality medical services tailored to the needs of our community. We strive to:

Deliver advanced diagnostics, treatment, and surgical care across multiple specialties.
Ensure personalized attention and compassionate support to every patient.
Promote preventive healthcare by spreading awareness and encouraging early diagnosis.
Our Vision

To be recognized as a trusted leader in multi-speciality healthcare, setting benchmarks in quality, innovation, and patient satisfaction. At Lamba Multi-Speciality Hospital, we envision a future where:

Healthcare is accessible and affordable to all, ensuring no one is left behind
Patients receive world-class treatment with cutting-edge technology and compassionate care.
Our hospital becomes the preferred choice for comprehensive medical care, from routine check-ups to critical procedures.
We continue to expand and evolve, staying ahead in medical advancements and delivering exceptional outcomes.
